Welcome to Faded, a mystery game designed, developed, and illustrated by Kyle Schaeffer.

What do I do?

The game is very simple. Each chapter in the game is a single page. You'll have to examine what you see on the page, interact with things by clicking and typing, and solve puzzles by piecing the clues you find together.

How do I save my progress?

Each chapter has a unique and secret web address (URL). You can save your progress at each chapter by using your browser's “bookmark” feature.

No one likes a spoiler. Please refrain from posting any information, URLs, or answers that would compromise anyone's ability to experience the mystery of Faded to its full and original intent. Thank you for your discretion!
